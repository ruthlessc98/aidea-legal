ORTAS Content Policy

Effective Date: December 19, 2024

Our Philosophy

ORTAS is a personal note-taking and organization tool. You own your content, and we respect your privacy. However, we must maintain a safe, legal platform for all users.

Acceptable Use

ORTAS is designed for:

  • Personal notes and ideas
  • Project planning and task management
  • Creative writing and brainstorming
  • Business notes and meeting records
  • Educational content and study notes
  • Personal journals and reflections

Prohibited Content

You may NOT use ORTAS to store:

Illegal Content

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) - zero tolerance, reported to NCMEC
  • Content promoting terrorism or violent extremism
  • Instructions for creating weapons, explosives, or dangerous substances
  • Stolen data, hacked information, or trade secrets
  • Content that violates export control laws

Harmful Content

  • Content promoting self-harm or suicide
  • Content intended to harass, threaten, or abuse others
  • Malware, viruses, or malicious code
  • Spam or automated abuse content

Infringing Content

  • Copyrighted material you don't have rights to use
  • Unauthorized use of trademarks
  • Content that violates others' privacy rights
  • Trade secrets or confidential information belonging to others

Other Prohibited Content

  • Content created primarily to circumvent our AI routing limits
  • Bot-generated spam or abuse
  • Content that violates our Terms of Service

Enforcement

How We Detect Violations

  • We do NOT actively monitor your private notes
  • We may investigate if:
    • You share a note publicly and it's reported
    • Automated systems detect potential CSAM
    • We receive a valid legal request
    • Abuse patterns are detected (e.g., excessive API usage)

Consequences

Violations may result in:

  1. Warning: First-time minor violations
  2. Content Removal: Specific prohibited content deleted
  3. Feature Restriction: AI routing disabled temporarily
  4. Account Suspension: Temporary loss of access (7-30 days)
  5. Account Termination: Permanent ban for serious violations
  6. Legal Action: Reported to authorities for illegal content

Appeals

If you believe enforcement action was incorrect:

  • Email: novasapient@proton.me
  • Include: Account email, date of action, explanation
  • We review appeals within 5-7 business days

Privacy and Content Access

When We Access Your Content

We only access your content when:

  • You explicitly grant us permission (e.g., for support requests)
  • Required by valid legal process (court order, subpoena)
  • Necessary to detect illegal content (CSAM, terrorism)
  • Investigating reported violations

What We See

  • Normal operation: We do NOT read your notes
  • AI routing: Only processes content temporarily, not stored by us
  • Support requests: Only if you send us content for troubleshooting
  • Legal requests: Only specific content identified in court orders

Copyright and DMCA

Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA)

If you believe content in ORTAS infringes your copyright:

Send a DMCA notice to: novasapient@proton.me

Include:

  1. Your physical or electronic signature
  2. Identification of the copyrighted work
  3. Description of where the infringing content is located
  4. Your contact information (address, phone, email)
  5. Statement of good faith belief that use is unauthorized
  6. Statement that the information is accurate and you're authorized to act

We will:

  • Respond within 48 hours
  • Remove infringing content if valid
  • Notify the user who posted it
  • Terminate repeat infringers

Counter-Notice

If your content was removed and you believe it was a mistake:

Send a counter-notice to: novasapient@proton.me

Include:

  1. Your physical or electronic signature
  2. Identification of removed content and its location
  3. Statement under penalty of perjury that removal was a mistake
  4. Your contact information
  5. Consent to jurisdiction of federal court

We will restore content in 10-14 business days unless the copyright owner files a court action.

Content Backup and Deletion

Your Responsibilities

  • Back up important content: We provide sync, but you should export critical notes
  • We are not a backup service: Use ORTAS for active note-taking, not long-term archival
  • Deletion is permanent: Once you delete content or your account, it cannot be recovered

Our Responsibilities

  • Weekly automated backups (for disaster recovery)
  • Backups are not accessible to individual users
  • Backups are deleted after 90 days
  • We make best efforts but do not guarantee data preservation

Content Ownership

  • You own your notes: All content you create belongs to you
  • Limited license: You grant us permission to store and process your content to provide the service
  • No other use: We do not use your content for marketing, AI training, or other purposes
  • Export anytime: You can download all your data in JSON format

Reporting Violations

If you discover prohibited content or behavior:

Email: novasapient@proton.me

Include:

  • Your contact information
  • Description of the violation
  • Link or screenshot (if applicable)
  • Date/time you discovered it

We will:

  • Investigate within 24-48 hours
  • Take appropriate action if violation confirmed
  • Notify you of the outcome (privacy permitting)

Transparency

We are committed to transparency about content moderation:

  • We publish annual transparency reports
  • We disclose the number of:
    • Violations detected
    • Accounts suspended/terminated
    • Legal requests received
    • DMCA notices processed

We will notify you via:
