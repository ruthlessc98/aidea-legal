Introduction

ORTAS ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ORTAS (the "App").

By using ORTAS, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address (for authentication)
  • User Content: Notes, projects, subjects, and any text content you create in the App
  • AI Context Descriptions: Optional descriptions you provide to help AI routing
  • Subscription Information: Payment details processed through Google Play (we do not store credit card information)

Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: AI routing decisions, note creation timestamps, feature usage
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, app version
  • Performance Data: Crash reports, error logs (anonymized)

Information from Third Parties

  • Google Play: Subscription status and purchase verification
  • Authentication: Email verification through Supabase Auth

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide Core Services: Store and sync your notes across devices
  • AI Routing: Analyze note content to automatically organize into appropriate projects
  • Improve Accuracy: Learn from your routing patterns to improve AI suggestions
  • Subscription Management: Process payments and manage access to premium features
  • App Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and fix bugs
  • Customer Support: Respond to your requests and provide assistance
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with applicable laws and regulations

AI Processing

How AI Routing Works

  • Your note content is sent to OpenAI's GPT-4o-mini model for routing analysis
  • AI analyzes semantic meaning to determine appropriate project/subject placement
  • Routing decisions are logged for transparency and pattern learning
  • We do not use your content to train AI models

Data Sent to OpenAI

  • Note content (only for active routing requests)
  • Project and subject names
  • Recent note titles and previews (for context)
  • OpenAI processes this data but does not retain it (per OpenAI's API terms)

Pattern Learning

  • After 20+ successful routes with similar keywords, the system uses patterns instead of AI
  • This reduces costs and improves speed while maintaining accuracy
  • Patterns are stored locally to your account and never shared

Data Storage and Security

Where Your Data Lives

  • Primary Database: Supabase (encrypted PostgreSQL database)
  • Location: Hosted on secure cloud infrastructure (AWS)
  • Backups: Automated weekly backups with 30-day retention

Security Measures

  • All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Database access requires authentication
  • Row-level security policies restrict data access to account owners only
  • API keys and secrets stored securely (never in code)
  • Regular security audits and updates
