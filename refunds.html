ORTAS Refund Policy

Last Updated: December 19, 2024

Our Commitment

We want you to be satisfied with ORTAS. If you're not happy with your purchase, here's what you need to know about refunds.

Refund Eligibility

✅ Eligible for Refund

  • First-time purchases within 48 hours with a valid reason
  • Technical failures that prevent you from using core features
  • Billing errors or unauthorized charges
  • Service disruption lasting more than 72 hours

❌ NOT Eligible for Refund

  • Refund requests made after 48 hours of purchase
  • Change of mind after actively using the service
  • Failure to cancel auto-renewal (you had control over this)
  • Dissatisfaction with AI accuracy (it's probabilistic, not perfect)
  • Account suspension due to Terms of Service violations

How to Request a Refund

  1. Email us: contactheaideapp@gmail.com
  2. Include:
    • Your email address registered with ORTAS
    • Google Play order number
    • Date of purchase
    • Reason for refund request
  3. Timeline: We respond within 3-5 business days
  4. Processing: Approved refunds are issued via Google Play (5-10 business days)

Google Play Refunds

You can also request refunds directly through Google Play:

  • Within 2 hours of purchase: Instant refund available
  • After 2 hours: Submit refund request through Play Store → Account → Purchase History
  • Google Play's refund policies apply

Subscription Cancellations

Important: Canceling your subscription is NOT the same as requesting a refund.

  • Cancellations prevent future charges but do NOT refund the current period
  • You retain access until the end of your paid period
  • To cancel: Google Play → Subscriptions → ORTAS → Cancel

Partial Refunds

We do not offer pro-rated or partial refunds for:

  • Mid-month cancellations
  • Unused AI routing credits
  • Time remaining on annual subscriptions

Free Trials

  • Free trials can be canceled anytime without charge
  • You must cancel before the trial ends to avoid being charged
  • No refunds for trial-to-paid conversions if you forgot to cancel
