Data Deletion Instructions

How to Delete Your Account and Data:

  1. Open ORTAS app
  2. Go to Settings (gear icon)
  3. Scroll to bottom
  4. Tap 'Delete Account'
  5. Confirm deletion

Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

Alternatively, email us at: novasapient@proton.me with subject 'Delete My Account' and we will process your request within 5 business days.
